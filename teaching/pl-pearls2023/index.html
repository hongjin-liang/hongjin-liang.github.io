The First Workshop on Programming Language Pearls (PL-Pearls 2023)

The goal of the workshop on Programming Language Pearls (PL-Pearls) is to provide an informal forum for understanding interesting ideas, theories, and techniques about programming languages.

The workshop speakers are the students who enroll in the course "The Light of Science (Young Scholars) â€” Programming Language Pearls" in the current year. Each speaker will submit an extended abstract, and give a short talk.

No formal proceedings will be published. The extended abstracts and the slides will be available online.

Important Dates

  • Extended abstracts submission deadline: 12:00 noon, Friday 15 December, 2023
  • Slides submission deadline: 12:00 noon, Friday 22 December, 2023 (tentative)
  • Workshop: Saturday 23 December, 2023 (tentative)

Submissions should be sent to the course instructor Hongjin Liang by email.

Guidelines for Submitting Extended Abstracts

Each extended abstract should be submitted in PDF format, and have no more than 2 pages of text (excluding bibliography). It should use the SIGPLAN format (specifically, the acmart LaTeX class with sigplan option), which is a two-column page layout with 10pt font. The LaTeX template can be found at the SIGPLAN author information page.

The extended abstract must be in English, and not anonymous.

Guidelines for Talks

Each talk will be 10 minutes long (including questions).
